What Cotopia Offers

Exclusive Products

Distributor Success Framework

  1. Stage 1 — Lead → Application — Applicant submits the Distributor Application and receives Intro Kit (NDA/EULA).
  2. Stage 2 — Compliance Completion — Applicant signs NDA and receives the Onboarding Kit.
  3. Stage 3 — Activation — Applicant signs Master Partner Agreement + Exhibits, submits tax docs, and pays activation fee.
  4. Stage 4 — Brand Deployment — Distributor receives Brand Assets Kit: flyers, graphics, posting tips, product catalog, content calendar, templates.
  5. Stage 5 — Growth & Scaling — Access to compensation plan, team-building systems, analytics, support, and leadership path.

Compensation Structure Overview

  • Retail Margin – Earn profit on direct sales.
  • Activation Bonus – Earn on each distributor activated under your line.
  • Residual Override – Earn monthly on the production of your team.
  • Leadership Rewards – Tiered bonuses for leaders who build strong, compliant teams.
  • Brand Equity Access – Access to exclusive product releases and regional expansion opportunities.
